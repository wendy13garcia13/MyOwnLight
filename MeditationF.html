Transmutation

The act of changing something into a different form, substance, or nature. Transmuting a damaging energy into a healing energy.

Self Reflection

The process of examining yourself, your behaviors, and your attitudes. Some action or circumstance upset me. So I self reflect on what role my actions, feelings or trauma played in how the action or circumstance made me feel.

Healing

The process of restoring health. How do I work through the trauma, change the actions, or control or work through the feelings.

Protection

Energy protection, through behavior and actions.
